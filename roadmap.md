# Teams

Display available teams, by:
    - a filtered list pre-provided, or (less rate limit heavy)
    - have filter options for the entire list, or (rate limit heavy, prone to many issues the hour before reset)
    - do not display at all, auto-join based on information previously provided (little rate limit impact, little chance of issues arising, but less freedom)
        - Freedom will always be provided in other ways, just not as easily accessible (through commands instead of a reaction message).
    - Incorporate both, non-display being the default?
        - How to avoid rate limit/accurate information issues when the ladder is only hours away if the display is always available?
        - How to handle teams at reset which are not full but have already began running the game and do not want more players? Allow team mates to close the team off?
Setup system for designated character role
    - Have to be approved by other team members to switch characters?
    - Do we enforce a limit of a character, and allow overrides?
Vote kicking system?
    - Perhaps it requires moderator permission or just initiated by a moderator?
        - Establish a mod command for kicking a player from a team and potentially preventing rejoin?
Team switching/leaving system
    - Having such a system (which is almost necessary), makes having a list of teams to join more difficult, as teams listed to a player have the potential to be inaccurate
    - How does team switching come about if no list is provided?
        - It may be risky to require those that wish to play together to create custom teams to do so
Private-like team system available to the public to some degree?
    - Raises the issue of listing the teams, as each team will be different
    - This raises a lot of complicated issues in terms of what people want, as too much customization can become an issue in allowing people to be situated
